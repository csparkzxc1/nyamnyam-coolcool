## Long description (≤4,000 chars)

> Stop staring at the clock.
> NyamNyam CoolCool predicts your baby's next feed and nap, and reminds you ahead of time.

Made for new moms in their first six post-partum weeks. This isn't a logging app — it's a **prediction app**. One tap is enough. We learn your baby's rhythm and notify you 10 minutes before the next feed.

### Highlights

- 🍼 **Next-feed prediction** — Blends Korean MOHW (아이사랑), AAP, and AASM standards with your baby's last 7-day pattern.
- 😴 **Sleep-cue head's-up** — Reminds you 15 minutes before the predicted nap window so you can settle the baby before overtired kicks in.
- ⚠️ **Safety alerts** — Auto-detects the newborn 4-hour feeding rule, low 24-h wet diaper count, and the 1,000 ml daily formula ceiling. In-app banner + critical-channel push.
- 👨‍👩‍👧 **Family sharing** — Spouses or grandparents can join the same baby in real time, so handoffs are seamless.
- 📈 **WHO growth chart** — Plots the baby's weight against WHO P3 / P50 / P97 percentile bands.
- 📚 **Age-banded guide + FAQ** — Sleep-cue list, 10-question FAQ written for first-time parents.

### Designed for tired hands

- Start a feed with one tap. Stop it with one tap. Time and amount auto-save.
- Breast / formula / mixed / solid — every feeding pattern works.
- Diaper, bath, sleep, growth — same instant flow.
- Made a mistake? Long-press the timeline dot to edit.

### Safety & privacy

- All data lives in your account only. Row-Level Security enforced server-side.
- Only caregivers you invited can see records.
- No third-party analytics on personal data.

### Medical disclaimer

This app provides general guidance, not medical diagnosis. If your baby's condition is concerning or warnings recur, please consult a pediatrician. For postpartum mental-health support in Korea, dial 1577-0199.
